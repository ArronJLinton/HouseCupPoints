House Point Hunt



  • House Study Group 5 (+1) points
    • Minimum of 3 students from the same house.
    • 5 points for the first 3 students and 1 point for each additional student from the same house.
    • Can be earned an unlimited number of times per week.
    • Selfie must be posted on #house-cup channel with each member tagged.
  • Mixed House Study Group 2+ points
    • Minimum of 4 students from any house.
    • If the majority of the students are from one house, they will be counted as a House Study Group.
    • 2 points per person awarded to their house.
    • Can be earned an unlimited number of times per week.
    • Selfie must be posted on #house-cup channel with each member tagged.
  • Remote Study Group 1+ points
    • Minimum of 3 students.
    • 1 point per student, regardless of house.
    • Can be earned an unlimited number of times per week.
    • Picture or screencap of students in session must be posted on #house-cup channel with each member tagged.
  • Stack Overflow Questions (Points increased!)5 points
    • Stack Overflow is a community where developers ask and answer questions for each other. Earn points for every correct answer you provide to others.
    • 3 points per correct answer on Stack Overflow.
    • Can be earned an unlimited number of times.
    • Link to your answer must be posted on #house-cup. Clarify which username is yours, if necessary.
  • Bookstore Selfie 3 points
    • Take a selfie at a bookstore reading a coding book.
    • You must read a chapter of the book and provide a few sentences summarizing what you've learned.
    • Can be earned only once per student.
    • Selfie must be posted on #house-cup channel.
  • Weekly Highlight Email 2 points
    • Email a highlight of the week to Jen.
    • Highlight must be an acheivement, obstacle you overcame, or something new you learned.
    • Can be earned once per week, per student.
  • Meetup Selfie (Points increased!)15+ points
    • Take a selfie at a coding meetup.
    • Selfies must be posted in #house-cup channel with the name and location of the meetup.
    • Can be earned an unlimited number of times per week per student.
    • Selfies are worth 15 points per student. Be sure to tag everyone in the selfie or post separately.
  • Open Source PR (Points increased!)15 (+15) points
    • Make a pull request (PR) on an open source project.
    • Must be an additional PR, not one that has been submitted for homework.
    • 10 points earned for making a PR.
    • Additional 10 point bonus if the PR is merged.
    • Post the PR URL in the #house-cup channel.
  • House Pride 2 points
    • Wear your house colors to class
      • GryffinDOM - Red
      • RavenClosure - Blue
      • GitHubblePuff - Yellow
      • SlytherIndentYourCode - Green
    • Must be shirt, sweatshirt, sweater, etc - Socks and Jeans don't count!
    • Can be earned once per class, per student
  • GitHub Commit Streak 10 (+10) points
    • Make at least 1 GitHub commit per day, for a minimum of 14 days.
    • Each additional day will earn you an extra 2 points.
    • Additional 5 point bonus for 5+ commits per day, for a minimum of 14 days.
  • Write a Blog Post 5 (+10) points
    • Must be a minimum of 250 words, plus code and/or pictures.
    • Can be earned 2x per week, per student.
    • Additional 10 point bonus if blog article is published on one of the following sites:
      • DEV
      • Medium

    • Example of a more elaborate Rube Goldberg Machine
  • Harry Potter Flash Mob 5+ points
    • Minimum of 3 people needed.
    • Additional 1 bonus point per broom in the dance routine, with a maximum of 10 brooms.
  • Hackathon Participation 35+ points
    • Must be an advertised and hosted JavaScript hackathon event.
    • Additional 35 points awarded for each student participant.
